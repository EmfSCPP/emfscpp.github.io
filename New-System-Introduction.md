#A New System

The current system of governance, education, and society is
enough to live in, but not enough to thrive in. A new system that can overcome these flaws
would be marvelous. Unfortunately, it's likely possible that we may not save everyone,
it's likely that a small portion of people may be left behind. For every winner,
there are losers. Powerful AI may be able to solve these issues.
Infact, current AI is showing the cracks of our current system, like how
students use ChatGPT to generate essays, do homework, record and summarise notes
and so on. Even teachers are using AI, at that point, the only reason a teacher is in school
is probably just to read lectures aloud, lectures made by AI. This will soon
jump to other areas like governance and justice, maybe, by the time you're
reading this, there may have an AI-related issue in these areas.

We conclude that as things change rapidly, we have to change our systems as well,
however, we cannot expect all good from companies and government, it's our
duty to let them know that these decisions have consequences, and that we are
not simply ignorant.
