# Introduction

The future may be wonderful and harmonious, with great advances
or terrible with non-existent privacy, corruption, and death.
As citizens, we can promote the right things, they are difficult to find
but humanity's welfare is benefitted.
At the center of the future is Artificial Intelligence, an imitation that is 
able to surpass human capabilities, and supersede us in the future.
There is so much pressure - autonomy risks, psychosis, cognitive decline, security risks
Yet there is so much potential - biology, technology, peace, interplanetary travel, mysteries solved.
AI is the way forward, but it must be traversed carefully 
like a teenager navigating his environment and his peers.
With combined effort, we can mature into an adult.
Current times are not ideal, mass wealth concentration, government corruption, wars, crime.
And singing lullabies at night won't fix these issues.
There is so much preventing us, but we are alive to take logical* decisions.
I trust humanity, it has produced some terrible monsters, but that is not 99%.

*By logical, it means what is beneficial for humanity in general in an efficient and semi-permanent manner.
An example is targeting the education system, though insurmountable, it could solve many issues
